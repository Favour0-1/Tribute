About Late Akilo Agbebaku Micheal

Late Akilo Agbebaku Micheal

was a 400 level accounting student in the University of Benin.
He was the Deputy Head of Department shorten as DHO of Counselling and follow-up Department in the Redeemed Christian Fellowship (RCF UNIBEN/UBTH).
He is such a diligent worker in the house of the Lord. He is a young Leader conversant with the word of God, he is a peaceful and gentle being

Late DHO Agbebaku always encourage counselling and follow-up department to be active in following up their counselees,
even he thought us a code which is "48 120 2", that is 48hours(2 days), 120hours(5 days) and 2weeks(14 days).
The code is a guideline to communicate with our counselees, within 48hours, we should have reach out to our counselees once
within 120hours, we should have called twice and pay a visit once and within 2weeks, we should have called at least thrice and pay them visit twice.
The essence of the code is to ensure there is a close relationship between us the counsellors and our counselees, for them to be able to open up to us.
